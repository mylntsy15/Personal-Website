Let's Talk Hobby!

Welcome to my world of words, where every page turn is an adventure waiting to unfold! I'm shamelessly addicted to novels, finding solace, laughter, and tears within their pages. From solving mysteries with cunning detectives to exploring enchanted forests alongside brave heroes, each story whisks me away to new realms of imagination.

But it's not just the stories themselves that mesmerize me; it's the power of imagination they ignite. Through authors' eyes, I've learned empathy, courage, and the value of hope.

Now, let me share some of my favorite gems from my reading journey with you!

Psst! Want to dive into a world of wonder? Just give the book a click, it'll magically transport you to the bookstore where you can snag it to yourself.

  • "Will You Stay?" by Norhafsah Hamid iis a heartfelt story set in modern Malaysia, following Aisha and Haris as they grapple with love, loss, and the complexities of life. As they confront their pasts and uncertain futures, they must decide whether to fight for love or let go. Hamid's compelling narrative delves into themes of family, culture, and personal identity with depth and emotion.
  • "Twisted Love" by Anna Huang is a riveting tale of love, betrayal, and unexpected twists. Follow the gripping journey of two lovers as they navigate through secrets and challenges, culminating in a shocking revelation that will leave you breathless.
  • "The Seven Husbands of Evelyn Hugo" by Taylor Jenkins Reid is a captivating tale that follows the glamorous and enigmatic Evelyn Hugo, a Hollywood icon, as she reveals the secrets of her tumultuous life to a young journalist named Monique. Through Evelyn's narrative, we journey through her seven marriages, each one unveiling layers of ambition, love, betrayal, and sacrifice. As Monique delves deeper into Evelyn's past, she discovers unexpected connections that will forever change both of their lives.
  • "It Ends with Us" by Colleen Hoover is a gripping tale that delves into the complexities of love, sacrifice, and resilience. Through its compelling narrative, we follow Lily Bloom as she navigates the highs and lows of a tumultuous relationship, ultimately confronting her past and making decisions that will shape her future. This emotionally charged story challenges societal norms and leaves readers pondering the true meaning of courage and forgiveness.
  • "It Starts With Us" by Colleen Hoover where love, resilience, and tough choices collide. This gripping novel explores the complexities of relationships, revealing the strength found in unexpected places. Join Lily as she navigates the tangled web of her past, present, and future, in a story that will leave you breathless and profoundly moved.
  • "A Million Kisses in Your Lifetime" by Monica Murphy is a heartwarming tale that follows the journey of Lily and Jack, childhood friends turned soulmates. Set against the backdrop of a quaint seaside town, their story unfolds with every shared moment, laughter, and tear. As they navigate life's twists and turns, their love blossoms, proving that even amidst life's chaos, a million kisses are never enough when shared with the one you love.
