About me

A girl proudly born in Negeri Sembilan on November 15, 2023. She's the dynamic middle child in a trio of siblings, whose personality dances between introversion with strangers and effervescent extroversion with her cherished inner circle. With a knack for humor that could rival a stand-up comedian, she effortlessly brings smiles to everyone's faces. You'll often find her immersed in the world of movies, sitcoms, and novels, indulging her voracious appetite for entertainment and storytelling.

A maestro of multitasking, she abhors procrastination like a cat detests water. Her heart beats fervently for web development, a passion that drives her to constantly seek knowledge and glean insights from fellow enthusiasts. Ever the adventurous spirit, she thrives on diving headfirst into new experiences and mastering fresh skills at every turn.
